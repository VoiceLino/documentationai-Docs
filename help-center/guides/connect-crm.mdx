## Overview

Sync your CRM to VoiceLine for sales insights.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Integrations">
    In VoiceLine dashboard, click "Integrations" in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your CRM">
    Choose Salesforce, HubSpot, or another supported CRM.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize access">
    Sign in to your CRM and approve access when prompted.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sync contacts">
    Click "Sync". Contacts will be imported automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

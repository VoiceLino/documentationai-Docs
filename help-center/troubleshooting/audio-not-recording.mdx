---
title: Audio Not Recording
description: Resolving common VoiceLine audio input issues.
---

## Symptoms

- Voice notes do not save
- No waveform appears when recording

## Causes

- Microphone permission not enabled
- Browser incompatibility

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check microphone access">
    Open settings > Privacy > Enable microphone for your browser/app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refresh browser">
    Try reloading the page or restarting your app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try another browser">
    Supported: Chrome, Edge, Firefox (latest versions).
  </Step>
</Steps>

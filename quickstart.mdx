## Prerequisites

Before starting, ensure you have:

<Callout kind="info">
- A sales CRM account (Salesforce, HubSpot, or similar)
- A mobile device running iOS 15+ or Android 10+
- An active email address for verification
- Permissions to connect your CRM (admin access recommended)
</Callout>

VoiceLine works seamlessly with popular CRMs to log calls automatically and provide AI-powered insights.

## Create and Verify Your Account

Sign up in under 2 minutes to access the AI assistant platform.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the signup page" icon="external-link">
    Go to `https://app.getvoiceline.com/signup` and enter your email, name, and company details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification link from VoiceLine. Click it to activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your password" icon="lock">
    Return to the app and create a strong password. Enable two-factor authentication for security.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Use your work email for seamless CRM integration later.
</Callout>

## Download the Mobile App

VoiceLine's mobile app is essential for field sales capture. Download based on your device.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="iOS" icon="apple">
    Open the App Store, search for "VoiceLine", and tap **Get**.

    ```
    Minimum requirements: iOS 15.0, 100MB storage
    ```
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Android" icon="android">
    Open Google Play Store, search for "VoiceLine", and tap **Install**.

    ```
    Minimum requirements: Android 10.0, 100MB storage
    ```
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

Log in with your verified credentials after installation.

## Configure CRM Connection

Connect your CRM to auto-log activities and unlock analytics.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open app settings" icon="settings">
    Tap the profile icon, then **Integrations**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your CRM" icon="database">
    Choose Salesforce, HubSpot, or Pipedrive from the list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize connection" icon="shield">
    Follow the OAuth prompts. Grant permissions for call logging and data sync.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Expandable title="Common CRM Setup Codes" default-open="false">
  For custom CRMs, use this webhook format:

  ```json
  {
    "webhook_url": "https://api.getvoiceline.com/webhooks/crm-sync",
    "secret": "YOUR_WEBHOOK_SECRET"
  }
  ```
</Expandable>

## Record Your First Sales Conversation

Capture your next call to generate instant AI insights.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start a new capture" icon="mic">
    Tap the **+** button and select **Record Call**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Conduct the call" icon="phone">
    Place your sales call. VoiceLine transcribes and analyzes in real-time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review insights" icon="zap">
    End the call. View summary, key topics, and action items automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Expect `<1 minute` processing time for insights like sentiment analysis and next steps.

## Access the Dashboard

View aggregated insights across your team.

1. Log into `https://dashboard.getvoiceline.com`.
2. Navigate to **Analytics** for call trends.
3. Check **Coaching** for rep performance tips.

<Callout kind="success">
  Congrats! You've captured your first conversation. AI insights are now available.
</Callout>
